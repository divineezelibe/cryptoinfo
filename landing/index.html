About Us

Our cryptocurrency portfolio project was born out of a personal journey into the world of digital assets and investments. Inspired by the ever-evolving blockchain space and the desire to simplify cryptocurrency management, we embarked on this project to create a user-friendly solution.

The journey started in [2022] as part of our educational journey at ALX & Holberton School in partnership with Master Card. This project is a testament to our dedication to mastering the art of software development while addressing real-world challenges.

Key Features

Real-Time Portfolio Overview

Get a comprehensive real-time overview of your cryptocurrency portfolio in one place. See the current market value, the percentage change in portfolio value, and individual coin prices.

Diverse Coin Tracking

Track a wide range of cryptocurrencies, including Bitcoin, Ethereum, and many altcoins. Easily add, remove, or edit holdings for different coins in your portfolio.

Performance Analytics

Access detailed performance analytics and historical data for each cryptocurrency in your portfolio.
